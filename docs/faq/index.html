Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with OS SEO — pricing, process, timelines, and what to expect.

What SEO services does OS SEO offer?+

OS SEO offers technical SEO audits, content strategy, link building, local SEO, AI-powered SEO, and international SEO. Each service can be delivered standalone or combined into a full SEO program depending on your goals.

How much do your SEO services cost?+

Pricing depends on the service and scope — technical audits start at $450 one-time, while ongoing content and SEO programs typically start around $600–$1,800 per month. Every project starts with a free consultation to scope exact pricing for your situation.

How long does it take to see SEO results?+

Technical fixes can show crawl and indexing improvements within weeks. Content and link building typically take 3–6 months to mature in rankings, as this depends on competition, domain authority, and how consistently the strategy is executed.

Do you work with businesses outside your home country?+

Yes — OS SEO works with clients across Europe, Australia, and the Arab world, and supports both English and Arabic content and communication.

Do you offer one-time projects or only ongoing contracts?+

Both. Technical SEO audits are typically one-time projects, while content strategy, link building, and local SEO are usually structured as ongoing monthly engagements since SEO compounds over time.

Can you guarantee first-page Google rankings?+

No ethical SEO provider can guarantee specific rankings, since Google's algorithm is outside any agency's control. What we guarantee is a transparent, data-driven process that consistently improves the technical and content foundations rankings are built on.

What's included in a free audit?+

A free audit gives you a high-level review of your site's biggest technical and content opportunities, along with a recommendation on which service would have the most impact for your specific situation.

Which industries do you work with?+

We've worked across home services, local trade businesses, SaaS, ecommerce, and professional services, with experience adapting SEO strategy to industry-specific search behavior.
