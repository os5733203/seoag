GEO · AEO · 2026

Beyond Traditional SEO — GEO and AEO for Saudi Businesses

SEO across the Middle East is entering a far more complex phase in 2026. AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity are reshaping how Saudi users search, compare businesses, and consume information — and search visibility is becoming inseparable from information structure and AI retrieval.

GEO — Generative Engine Optimisation

What Is GEO?

GEO is the practice of structuring website content — in both Arabic and English — so it is cited and surfaced by AI systems including Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. AI Overviews now appear in approximately 45% of Saudi search results, meaning businesses whose content is not structured for AI extraction are increasingly invisible to a growing share of Saudi searchers.

AEO — Answer Engine Optimisation

What Is AEO?

AEO focuses on formatting content so AI systems can extract clear, citable answers — short Arabic and English definitions of 40 to 60 words, numbered lists, and comparison tables. These formats are the ones most likely to be extracted into Google's featured snippets for Arabic queries and into AI-generated answers across ChatGPT and Gemini.

Why It Matters in Saudi Arabia

The Saudi AI Search Reality

Many corporate websites in Saudi Arabia rely on vague positioning language, overloaded homepage sections, and confusing navigation — structures AI systems cannot reliably interpret or recommend. Businesses investing earlier into clearer content systems, sharper positioning, and AI-ready information architecture are gaining long-term visibility advantages as Vision 2030 accelerates digital adoption across the Kingdom.
