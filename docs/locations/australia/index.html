GEO · AEO · 2026

Beyond Traditional SEO — GEO and AEO for Australian Businesses

In 2026, ranking in blue links is no longer enough. Australian businesses need visibility across Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude — the platforms where 64% of Australian users now encounter their first answer.

GEO — Generative Engine Optimisation

What Is GEO?

GEO is the practice of structuring website content so it is cited, quoted, or surfaced by AI-powered search systems including Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity. In Australia, 64% of users now regularly encounter AI-generated answers in Google Search. Businesses whose content is not structured for AI extraction are increasingly invisible in these results, regardless of their traditional keyword rankings.

AEO — Answer Engine Optimisation

What Is AEO?

AEO focuses on formatting content so AI systems can extract clear, accurate, and citable answers from it. This means writing short definitional paragraphs, using structured FAQ sections, implementing schema markup, and ensuring every key question your Australian customers ask has a concise, factual answer on your website that an AI system can confidently cite.

Why It Matters in Australia

The Australian AI Search Reality

Australian publishers are reporting impressions doubling while organic clicks drop 50% or more as AI Overviews answer queries directly. Businesses that are cited within AI Overviews capture brand visibility even when users do not click. Being cited by Google AI, ChatGPT, or Perplexity for Australian local queries — "best plumber Sydney" or "dentist Melbourne" — is now a measurable brand asset independent of traditional click-through traffic.
