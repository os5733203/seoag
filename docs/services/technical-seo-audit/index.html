Pricing & Investment

What SEO actually costs, and how to budget realistically.

How much does SEO cost?+

Pricing depends on the service and scope. A standalone technical SEO audit starts at $450 as a one-time project. Ongoing programs — content strategy, link building, local SEO — typically start around $600 to $1,800 per month. Enterprise and multi-market projects are quoted individually after a free consultation. See exact tier pricing on each service page, starting with our Technical SEO Audit service.

How much should a small business budget for SEO?+

Most small businesses see meaningful traction starting at $600–$1,000 per month for a focused program such as local SEO or a single content track. Businesses competing in more saturated markets, or running multiple service lines at once, typically budget $1,800 or more per month for faster, more comprehensive movement.

Is SEO a one-time service or a monthly commitment?+

Both models exist side by side. Technical SEO audits are usually one-time projects with a clear, finite deliverable. Content strategy, link building, and local SEO are structured as ongoing monthly engagements, since organic visibility compounds with consistent, sustained effort rather than a single fix.

Timeline & Results

A realistic view of how fast SEO actually moves.

How long does it take to see SEO results?+

Technical fixes can show crawl and indexing improvements within a few weeks of implementation. Content and link building typically take 3 to 6 months to mature into stable rankings, depending on competition level, your existing domain authority, and how consistently the strategy is executed.

Why does SEO take longer than paid ads?+

Paid ads buy temporary visibility the moment a campaign goes live. SEO instead builds durable assets — technical infrastructure, content, and authority — that search engines need time to crawl, evaluate, and trust. The tradeoff is that SEO traffic keeps compounding long after the work is done, unlike ad spend.

How do you report on SEO progress?+

Clients receive regular reporting covering rankings, organic traffic, and completed deliverables, with a monthly or bi-weekly call depending on engagement size — so progress is never a black box and you always know exactly what's been done and why.

Working Together

What's expected from you, and what we handle.

What information do you need from me to get started?+

Typically just read-only access to Google Search Console and Analytics, a short discovery call to understand your business goals, and any existing brand or content guidelines you'd like us to follow. Everything else — research, strategy, and execution — is handled by our team.

What's included in a free audit?+

A free audit covers a high-level review of your site's biggest technical and content opportunities, along with a clear recommendation on which service would create the most impact for your specific situation — no obligation to proceed.

Can I cancel an ongoing SEO engagement?+

Yes. Monthly engagements run without long-term lock-in, so you can pause or end the engagement with notice as outlined in your agreement. We'd simply ask for the chance to understand any concerns first, since most issues can be resolved by adjusting scope or priorities.

Trust & Guarantees

What we can promise, and what no honest agency ever will.

Do SEO agencies guarantee first-page Google rankings?+

No ethical SEO provider can guarantee specific rankings, because Google's algorithm sits outside any agency's control — a position confirmed directly by Google's own Search Central guidance. What OS SEO guarantees instead is a transparent, data-driven process that consistently strengthens the technical and content foundations that rankings are actually built on.

Yes. OS SEO works with clients across Europe, Australia, and the Arab world, and supports both English and Arabic content, communication, and reporting throughout the engagement.
