Problems We Solve
Is Your Website Suffering From These?
These are the most common technical issues that prevent websites from ranking — and we fix all of them.
🐛
Crawl Errors & Blocked Pages
Googlebot can't crawl your pages, so they never get indexed. We identify and fix every crawl block, robots.txt error and noindex tag.
🐌
Slow Page Speed & Poor Core Web Vitals
Page speed is a Google ranking factor. We optimise LCP, FID and CLS scores to meet Google's standards and improve user experience.
📄
Duplicate Content & Canonical Issues
Duplicate pages split your ranking power. We consolidate content and implement correct canonical tags to concentrate your SEO authority.
🏗️
Poor Site Architecture
A confusing site structure wastes crawl budget and dilutes link equity. We redesign your URL structure and internal linking for maximum SEO impact.
📱
Mobile Usability Problems
Google uses mobile-first indexing. If your site fails on mobile, your rankings suffer. We audit and fix every mobile usability issue.
🔗
Broken Links & Redirect Chains
Broken links frustrate users and waste crawl budget. Redirect chains slow down your site. We find and fix every broken link and chain.

How the Technical SEO Audit Works
01

Discovery & Access

We gather access to Google Search Console, Google Analytics and your CMS. We run an initial crawl of your entire website to map its current state.

02

Deep Technical Analysis

Using Screaming Frog, Ahrefs, SEMrush and Google's own tools, we conduct a 200+ point technical analysis covering every ranking factor.

03

Report & Priority Roadmap

You receive a detailed audit report with every issue documented, its impact explained, and a prioritised fix list — ranked by what will move rankings fastest.
