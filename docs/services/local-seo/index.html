Our Process
How We Rank You in Google Maps
01

Local Audit & Competitor Research

We audit your current local presence, GBP profile, citation consistency and review profile — then analyse the top 3 competitors in the Google Maps pack to understand exactly what's needed to outrank them.

02

GBP Optimisation

We fully optimise your Google Business Profile — selecting the right primary and secondary categories, writing keyword-rich descriptions, adding photos, and setting up Google Posts and Q&A.

03

Citation Building & Cleanup

We build consistent citations across 60+ local directories, fix any existing NAP inconsistencies, and submit your business to all relevant industry-specific directories.

04

Review Generation Strategy

We set up a systematic review generation process — email/SMS sequences to encourage happy customers to leave reviews — dramatically improving your review count and rating.

05

Local Content & Link Building

We create location-specific landing pages optimised for local keywords and acquire local backlinks from community websites, chambers of commerce and local publications.
