Problems We Solve
Why Your Content Isn't Ranking
Publishing content without a strategy is the most common SEO mistake — and we fix all of these.
🔎
Wrong Keyword Targeting
You're writing about topics nobody searches for, or targeting keywords too competitive to rank for. We identify the right keywords with the perfect difficulty-to-volume ratio.
🏝️
Content Silos & No Architecture
Random blog posts with no internal linking strategy dilute your topical authority. We build pillar-cluster architectures that consolidate your SEO power.
📉
Thin or Generic Content
Google's Helpful Content system rewards depth and expertise. Short, generic articles that don't satisfy search intent get demoted — we create content that genuinely helps.
🚫
No Content Calendar or Consistency
Sporadic publishing confuses Google and fails to build momentum. A consistent, strategic content calendar is essential for compounding SEO growth.

Our Process
How We Build Your Content Strategy
01

Niche & Competitor Research

We research your niche thoroughly — understanding your target audience, mapping competitor content strategies, and identifying the white space opportunities your competition is missing.

02

Keyword Research & Mapping

We identify 100+ keyword opportunities across the funnel — from awareness-stage informational queries to decision-stage commercial keywords — and map them to specific content pieces.

03

Content Architecture Design

We build your pillar-cluster content structure — defining which articles will serve as pillar pages, which will be cluster articles, and how they interconnect through internal linking.

04

Content Creation

Our writers produce fully-optimised, long-form articles that are genuinely helpful, satisfy search intent, and are structured to rank. Every article includes proper headings, schema markup, and internal links.

We track the performance of every piece of content, refreshing underperforming articles and doubling down on what works. Monthly reports show ranking improvements, traffic growth and conversion data.

FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What is SEO content strategy?+
SEO content strategy is the process of researching keywords, planning content topics and creating optimised content that ranks on Google and drives organic traffic. It includes pillar-cluster architecture, keyword mapping and content calendars.
Do you write the content or just plan it?+
Both. We research keywords, build the content plan AND write the actual content. Each article is fully optimised for SEO including meta tags, headings, internal linking and schema markup.
How long before content starts ranking?+
New content typically takes 3-6 months to rank. Low-competition keywords can rank within weeks. Building a full topical authority typically takes 6-12 months of consistent publishing.
