Problems We Solve
International SEO Mistakes That Cost Rankings
🔀
Wrong or Missing Hreflang
Incorrect hreflang is one of the most common and costly international SEO errors. It causes Google to show the wrong language version to users — destroying your international rankings.
🌐
Poor URL Structure for International
Choosing the wrong international URL structure (ccTLDs vs subdirectories vs subdomains) can fragment your domain authority and make international expansion far harder.
🔄
Translated But Not Localised
Direct translations rank poorly because they don't match how local users actually search. Each market needs localised keyword research and content — not just translated copy.
📍
No Geo-Targeting Setup
Without correct geo-targeting signals, Google doesn't know which country to rank your pages for. We set up every signal correctly — hreflang, GSC geo-targeting and structured data.

FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What is hreflang and why does it matter?+
Hreflang is an HTML attribute that tells Google which language and country a page targets. Correct hreflang implementation ensures Google shows the right version of your content to the right audience — preventing duplicate content issues and improving rankings in each target market.
Should I use subdirectories, subdomains or ccTLDs?+
For most businesses, subdirectories (e.g. example.com/de/) are recommended as they consolidate domain authority. Subdomains and ccTLDs can work but require more resources. We assess your specific situation and recommend the best structure.
Do you work with Arabic and right-to-left languages?+
Yes. We have extensive experience with Arabic SEO and RTL languages. We handle technical RTL implementation, Arabic keyword research, Arabic content creation and MENA market-specific link building.
